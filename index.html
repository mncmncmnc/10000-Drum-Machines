! 10,000 Drum Machines !

about/submit

Back in 2019, I sketched out designs for a couple of web-based drum machines. I didn't actually code them until 2025 and then thought, ok, that was fun, I should make more.

Why 10,000? I don't know. I was trying to come up with a name for a collection of drum machines and "10,000 DRUM MACHINES" was all that came to mind.

I probably won't ever reach that lofty number (a life expectancy assumption of 75 years only gives me 13,182 days left), but maybe we can get closer if you help!

If you have an idea or a prototype for a drum machine that can live on the web, please reach out. I would love to feature it here <3
