Shape white, pink, and brown noise into sequence-able sounds, or let them drone...


This drum machine was inspired by white noise machines in medical offices, the use of noise in analog synthesis, the choreographic practice of Julia Antinozzi, and the amazing instruments designed by JMT Synth.


It is the fifth entry in the 10,000 Drum Machines project by Maxwell Neely-Cohen.
